Categories

IAM

Identity & Access Management

Employees lose access, get locked out, or have accounts compromised. Incode adds a verification layer to the moments that matter most, including account recovery, MFA reset, and privileged access requests.

CIAM

Customer Identity & Access Management

Consumer accounts are a primary target for takeover. Incode steps in at high-risk moments in the customer journey, such as onboarding, account changes, and step-up authentication, without disrupting the experience.

IGA

Identity Governance & Administration

Governance decisions are only as reliable as the identity behind them. Incode ensures the right person is confirmed before access is certified, provisioned, or escalated.

ITDR

Identity Threat Detection & Response

When a threat is detected, identity is usually the entry point. Incode closes the gap by triggering real-time re-verification when suspicious activity is flagged.

ITSM

IT Service Management

Social engineering attacks target help desk agents every day. Incode handles identity verification before a request is acted on, so your team never has to make that call themselves.

Messaging

Messaging & Collaboration

Sensitive workflows happen inside messaging tools. Incode lets teams trigger and complete identity verification without leaving the platform they are already working in.

Video Conferencing

Video Conferencing

AI-generated deepfakes make it easy to impersonate someone on a call. Incode verifies participant identity before they join, ensuring the person on screen is who they claim to be.

HRIS

Human Resource Information System

The moment someone joins the organisation is when identity risk is highest. Incode ensures the person who shows up on day one is the person you hired.

ATS

Applicant Tracking System

North Korean IT workers and other fraudulent applicants have made candidate identity a real risk. Incode confirms the candidate appearing in the hiring cycle is who they say they are.

CRM

Customer Relationship Management

Trigger identity verification directly from within your CRM workflows, adding assurance to customer service interactions where it matters most.

E-Commerce

E-Commerce

Selling age-restricted or high-value products online requires more than a checkbox. Incode verifies buyer identity and age at the point of purchase.

Age Verification

Age Verification

Help meet age-related compliance and regulatory requirements globally, with verification that holds up across jurisdictions and use cases.

Hardware

Hardware & Kiosk

For organisations that need identity confirmed in person, Incode works with physical document scanning hardware at kiosks and points of service.
