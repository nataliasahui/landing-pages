Why partner with Incode

Every enterprise workflow that matters — onboarding, access, recovery, compliance — now has an identity verification requirement at its core. Incode gives your customers that layer.

Identity is the most targeted attack surface in the enterprise. Every account takeover, synthetic identity, and fraudulent onboarding starts with a failure to verify the person on the other side. Incode partners are the companies fixing that — embedding verified, biometric-grade identity directly into the products their customers depend on.
