Introducing GovFaceMatch

The only solution that delivers higher conversion without compromising identity assurance

Built with state DMVs, enabling real-time biometric matching against official government records to confirm identity and stop AI-assisted fraud.

ID barcode scan + selfie

Users scan the back of their government ID and take a live selfie in two simple guided steps.

Verify against DMV records

The selfie is matched to the issuing DMV's official record within a secure environment.

Real-time result

A verification result is returned instantly, delivering a fast, defensible, and auditable outcome.

Benefits

What changes when identity is verified against DMV records

Direct verification at the source transforms conversion and fraud performance.

  • Lower user friction

    Complete verification with a barcode scan and selfie. No document capture required.

  • Stronger protection

    Verify the person, not just the data. Stops synthetic identities and AI-assisted fraud that bypass traditional checks.

  • Built-in privacy

    Matching occurs within the DMV’s secure environment. No biometric data is stored and no government PII is shared.
